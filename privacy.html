Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how Laziza Food collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to Laziza Food

This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or mobile application. By using Laziza Food, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with transparency and care.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use our services:

  • Personal Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you register or place an order.
  • Order Information: Details of the items you order, payment method, and order history.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system for security and analytics purposes.
  • Location Data: Your delivery address or approximate location to provide accurate delivery services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to improve your experience with Laziza Food:

  • To process and deliver your food orders accurately and on time.
  • To send you order confirmations, updates, and support messages.
  • To personalise your experience and suggest menu items you may enjoy.
  • To improve our website, app, and overall services based on usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activity.

Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With delivery partners to fulfil your orders.
  • With payment processors to securely handle transactions.
  • When required by law or legal proceedings.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your account and personal data.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@lazizafood.com.
